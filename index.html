01What's in the giveaway

Five prompts in order. Each one builds on the last. You paste your partner context once at the top, then run them. The output is yours. Files you can hand to your team on Monday.

1
Pull from the source10-15 Build announcements grounded in your niche and your deals. Primary blogs only. Source-cited.
2
Sort: cool vs yoursBrutal split. Moonshots one side, "mine this quarter" the other. Honesty over enthusiasm.
3
Customer conversationThree conversations ready for next week. Opening line, objection killed, concrete next step.
4
Build me an offerOne packaged three-week fixed-price partner offer with deliverables your team can actually ship.
5
Make me the sourceA LinkedIn post in your voice and a six-line email to one customer. No corporate buzzwords.
